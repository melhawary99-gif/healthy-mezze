# Greek Salad

## Description

A fresh and colorful Greek Salad made with ripe tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, bell peppers, red onion, Kalamata olives, creamy feta cheese, and a simple extra virgin olive oil dressing. This classic Mediterranean dish is quick to prepare, naturally gluten-free, and perfect as a light meal or side dish.

---

## Health Benefits

Greek Salad is packed with vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and heart-healthy fats. Fresh vegetables provide vitamin C and fiber, while olive oil contains beneficial monounsaturated fats. Feta cheese adds calcium and protein, making this salad both nutritious and satisfying.

---

## Ingredients

- 2 large tomatoes
- 1 cucumber
- 1 green bell pepper
- 1 small red onion
- 150 g feta cheese
- 16 Kalamata olives
- 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt to taste
- Black pepper to taste

---

## Instructions

1. Wash and prepare all vegetables.
2. Cut tomatoes into wedges.
3. Slice cucumber into thick rounds.
4. Slice bell pepper into strips.
5. Thinly slice the red onion.
6. Combine vegetables in a large serving bowl.
7. Add Kalamata olives.
8. Place feta cheese on top.
9. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.
10. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and black pepper.
11. Toss gently before serving.

---

## Chef's Tips

- Use ripe seasonal tomatoes for the best flavor.
- Always use high-quality extra virgin olive oil.
- Chill ingredients before serving.
- Add the feta at the end to keep its shape.

---

## Storage

Best enjoyed fresh.

Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to one day.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I make Greek Salad ahead of time?

Yes. Prepare the vegetables in advance and add the dressing just before serving.

### Can I use another cheese?

Traditional Greek Salad uses feta, but goat cheese also works well.

### Is Greek Salad healthy?

Yes. It is rich in vegetables, healthy fats, fiber, and antioxidants.
